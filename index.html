I laid the foundation for my creative journey by studying photography. Through my prior experience as a model, I gained insights into the creative and visual world, which further fueled my passion for graphic design. Self‐study and practical experience in various companies deepened my skills.

Painting, which has accompanied me since childhood, is not just a hobby, but an essential part of my life. This artistic passion flows seamlessly into my work and I combine my experience in photography, graphic design and painting to create a multi‐layered understanding of visual aesthetics and expression.
